FREQUENT QUESTIONS

How do I add a new product to the inventory?
You can add a new product by navigating to the inventory section and clicking on "Add Product". Fill in the required details and save.
How do I search for a product in the inventory?
Use the search bar at the top of the inventory page to find products by name or SKU.
How do I update the quantity of an existing product?
Click on the product in the inventory list, then edit the quantity field and save the changes.
Can I generate an inventory report?
Yes, go to the Reports section and select "Inventory Report" to generate and download it.
What do I do if I have a duplicate product?
You can merge duplicate products by selecting them and choosing the "Merge" option, or delete one if it's not needed.
